## 📂 Projects

### 🛒 End-to-End E-Commerce Analytics
> *34,500 transactions. 7,903 customers. Two years of data. One uncomfortable picture.*

A 6-notebook analytics pipeline that examines a $5.87M synthetic e-commerce business from six different angles — each notebook answering a question the business actually needs answered.

| Notebook | Question | Key Finding |
|---|---|---|
| NB01 — Data Engineering | Can we trust the data? | 17 behavioural features engineered. Mean order $170, median $56.82 — 3× gap signals outlier skew. |
| NB02 — Sales & Forecasting | What drives revenue? | Electronics = 56.6% of revenue. ARIMA MAPE 12.21%. Discounts net-positive at +75.1% LTV. |
| NB03 — Segmentation | Who are the customers? | 4 segments via k-means. HVAR (6% of customers) = 24.7% of revenue. |
| NB04 — Churn Prediction | Who is about to leave? | Data ceiling documented honestly (ROC-AUC 0.498). Return behaviour = 52.7% of model signal. |
| NB05 — Fraud Detection | Who is gaming the system? | $96,822 exposure identified. 232 High/Critical risk customers flagged. |
| NB06 — Cohort Retention | What is a new customer worth? | M+1 retention 15.2%. HVAR LTV $2,237 = 3.45× Loyal customers. |

**Stack:** Python · pandas · scikit-learn · statsmodels · Pandera · pytest · Plotly · YAML config · Parquet
